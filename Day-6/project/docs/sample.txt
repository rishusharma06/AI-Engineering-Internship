AI Engineering Bootcamp — Company Policy Document

REFUND POLICY
Students can request a refund within 7 days of enrollment.
Refunds are processed within 5 business days.
To request a refund, email support@bootcamp.com with your enrollment ID.
Partial refunds are not available after course materials are accessed.

ATTENDANCE POLICY
Students must attend at least 80% of all sessions.
Missing more than 3 consecutive days requires a valid reason.
All sessions are recorded and available for 30 days after the course ends.

ASSESSMENT POLICY
Assessments are conducted every Friday.
Each assessment is practical — no written exams.
Students must score at least 60% to proceed to the next week.
Assessment results are shared within 24 hours.

CAPSTONE PROJECT
Every student must complete a capstone AI project in Week 3.
The project must use at least RAG, one LangChain component, and a React frontend.
Projects are presented on the last day of the bootcamp.

CERTIFICATE
Certificates are issued within 7 days of course completion.
Students must complete all assessments and the capstone project.
Certificates include a unique verification code.
